In the two-phase region, (Î±+Î²)-phase is gotten from the heat treatment with different holding time and temperatures. The temperature is below the phase transition temperature. The main characteristics of (Î±+Î²)-phase microstructure are irregular shape of grains, continuous and discontinuous Î±-phase on the grain boundary, and many small secondary Î±-phases. The punctate, spherical, flakiness and short rod Î±-phase exists in intragranular[22]. However, all (Î±+Î²)-phase will be converted into Î²-phase when the heating temperature is higher than phase transition temperature. The size and shape of grains are not identical.
